Tirupati Near Temples

Kapila Theertham

Kapila Theertham is a famous Saivite Temple and Theertham, located at Tirupati in the Tirupati District of Andhra Pradesh, India.

Govindaraja Temple

Sri Govindarajaswamy Temple is an ancient Hindu-Vaishnavite temple situated at the heart of the Tirupati city in Tirupati district of Andhra Pradesh state in India.

Padmavathi Temple

Padmavathi Temple is the temple dedicated to Goddess Padmavathi or Alamelumanga, the consort of Lord Venkateswara. The temple is situated in Tiruchanur.

Kalyana Venkateshwara Temple

Sri Kalyana Venkateswaraswamy Templeis a Hindu-Vaishnavite temple situated at Narayanavanam, a town in Tirupati district of Andhra Pradesh state,India.

Kodandarama Temple

Sri Kodandaramaswamy Temple is one of the famous temples, after Tirumala Venkateswara Temple

ISKCON Temple

Popularly also known as Hare Rama Hare Krishna temple, Daily darshan at ISKCON Tirupati Temple starts from 4.15 am to 9 pm
